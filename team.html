MEET THE TEAM

We believe that there is an amazing amount to be learned by working in "real-world" web production development, alongside other like-minded young people of Kosova.

With this in mind we began to look into finding the best practices on how we can make this happen. Technology + Institutions = Techstitution! The project engages youth in democratic processes and learning of new software skills, in order to contribute to the formation of ‘smart institutions’ that deliver high-quality services to Kosovo citizens.

We've had an amazing journey coming to where we are today, and now we feel confident that we have somehow positively impacted our country with our dedication and hard work.
