About

Working together is where ideas take shape! The main goal of #Techstitution is to learn practical software engineering skills by developing technologies to digitize institutional platforms.

The project activities will include:
- Engage youth in democratic processes
- Learn new software skills
- Digital production for Institutions
- Smart Institutions

The project is from UNICEF Innovations Lab, funded by Austrian Development Agency.

Open Data Kosova & Girls Coding Kosova are implementing partners.

Open Data Kosovo will contribute technical expertise in the field of software engineering as well as in digital product development management and quality assurance. Furthermore, it will use its experience in organizing and implement youth engagement digital capacity building workshops to design the planned workshops and ensure their proper execution.

Girls Coding Kosova will provide technical support as well as access to their network of young women in technology. In order promote a multidisciplinary approach to problem-solving, select GCK members will serve as tech ambassadors disseminating concepts of how citizens involved in different sectors can benefit from and contribute to technology for social good.
